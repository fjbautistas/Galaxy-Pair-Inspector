🌌 How to classify
Galaxy Pair Inspector
You will see cutout images from the DESI Legacy Survey. Your task is to classify each object into one of 5 categories.

Swipe to learn each one →
✅ Confirmed Pair
Two galaxies physically close in space (small rp, similar redshift). They may show tidal distortion or a connecting bridge. Mark as Pair.
❌ False Positive
Two galaxies that appear close on the sky but are at very different redshifts — a projection effect, not a real pair. Mark as FP.
🔀 Possible Merger
Two nuclei within a shared envelope, or strong tidal tails indicating an advanced merger. The boundary with Pair is fuzzy — use your judgment. Mark as PM.
⭐ Confirmed Group
3 or more galaxies physically associated (same redshift, gravitationally bound). The app marks the closest members. Mark as Group.
👥 Possible Pair
You're looking at a group, but two members stand out as a close interacting pair within it. The group context is real, but the pair deserves special attention. Mark as PP.
Tips
Navigate: swipe left/right or use Prev/Next.
Sky Viewer: double-tap the image for full context.
Unsure? Use FP — better conservative than wrong.
